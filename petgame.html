Virtual Pet Game/Mood Tracker

Introduction

Starting off as a random idea for Cruzhacks 2021 (a 3-day hackathon hosted in Santa Cruz, California), my friends and I wanted to design a Tamagotchi-like game that also helps players keep track of their mental health and take steps to make improvements. The game's unlockable pets and rooms serve as an incentive for players to keep logging onto the game daily and record their moods throughout the day. Our project won a $2000 sponsorship from UC Santa Cruz to continue the project as a team of 4 to launch our innovation to the real world.

Role(s)

Game Developer & Designer

Date

Jan 2021

Target Audience

This game is targeted towards people who enjoy pet simulators and also suffer from mental illnesses such as depression and anxiety. The demographics that these groups have in common are female-identifying and children, teenagers, and young adults (particularly those who played pet simulator games as children and would give into the nostalgic appeal of the app).
